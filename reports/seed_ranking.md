| 6 | hold | 3.280 | MXene-loaded soft hydrogel, exploratory only | oxidation; ion or flake toxicity; poor long-term culture evidence |

## Gate Holds

- **MXene-loaded soft hydrogel, exploratory only**
  - cell_compatibility below 3: A material that cannot plausibly support neural cell viability should not be prioritized.
  - culture_stability below 3: The interface must remain stable in warm aqueous culture medium.
